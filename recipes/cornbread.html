Cornbread

Descripition

This Cornbread recipe is deliciously moist with actual corn kernals. This recipe only takes 20 minutes prep and 20 minutes cook time.

Ingredients

  • 5 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 cup fine or medium yellow ground cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 1/3 cup packed dark brown sugar
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 can of corn

Steps

  1. Melt 4 tablespoons butter, set aside to cool
  2. Whisk together the cornmeal, flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and brown sugar in a medium bowl until well combined
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, egg and 3 tablespoons of vegtable oil
  4. Pour and stir in milk mixture into dry ingredients
  5. Add in melted butter and stir until well combined
  6. Set the cornbread batter aside for 10 mins
  7. While waiting, preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  8. Coat the 9 inch baking pan with the remaining butter
  9. Add the batter to the baking pan
  10. Bake for 20 to 25 mins, or until the center is firm and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean
  11. Allow the cornbread to cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving

Enjoy!
