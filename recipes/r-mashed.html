Refined Mashed Potatoes

Description

This Refined Mashed Potatoes are smooth and creamy. Best served with steak or chicken.

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs Russet Potatoes (5-7 potatoes)
  • 3 sticks unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1.5 cup heavy cream
  • 0.5 teaspoon black pepper
  • 0.5 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon Oregano
  • 2 teaspoon Buttery Steakhouse Rub
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into 2 inches cubes
  2. Rince the potatoes with cold water twice
  3. Place the potatoes into a pot and fill the water until 1 inch above the potatoes
  4. Season the pot with lots of salts
  5. Potatoes will be done when you could push and knife or fork easily through
  6. Strain the potato and place on a wire rack
  7. Press the potatoes through a Potato Presser
  8. Heat up milk and heavy cream, but do not boil
  9. while waiting for the liquid mixture, cut up butter into cubes
  10. When the liquid mixture, add the butter to the potatoes a little at a time
  11. When all of the butter is mixed, slowly add the liquid mixture until well combined
  12. If potatoes are cold, place back on stove
  13. Season with black pepper, garlic salt, oregano, Buttery Steakhouse Rub, and salt to taste
  14. Put the potatoes on a serving place and Enjoy!
