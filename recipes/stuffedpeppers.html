Stuffed Bell Peppers

Description

These bell peppers are a delicious healthy meal. In this recipe we will be using beef, but it would be good with any meat substitute.

Ingredients

  • 2 bell peppers
  • 5 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 0.75 cups of water
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 1.5 oz couscous
  • 1.5 oz of tomato paste
  • 1 roma tomato
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Wash and dry all produce
  3. Halve bell peppers lengthwise; remove stems and seeds
  4. Place cut side down on baking sheet and drizzle with oil, season with salt and pepper
  5. Roast until brown and softened, 18 to 20 minutes
  6. While bell peppers roast, melt butter in small pot over medium heat
  7. Add couscous and a pinch of salt and pepper
  8. Cook while stirring until toasted, 2-3 minutes
  9. Stir in 3/4 cup of water, bring to boil
  10. Cover and reduce heat to low simmer until tender, 6-8 minutes
  11. Set aside with cover on
  12. Halve, peel and dice onion
  13. Finely dice tomato
  14. Heat a drizzle of oil in large, ovenproof, pan over medium heat
  15. Add oinion and cook until softened, 4-5 mins
  16. Add beef and season with salt and pepper
  17. Cook until browned, 4-6 minutes
  18. Stir in tomato paste and diced tomato in beef pan until well combined
  19. Add chicken stock, season with salt and pepper, cook until thickened,1-2 minutes
  20. Stir in couscous witil well combined
  21. Take off heat
  22. Once bell peppers are done, flip and stuff each with the filling
  23. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese
  24. Bake stuffed peppers until cheese has melted and Enjoy!
